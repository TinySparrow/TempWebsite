What We Do

Sparrow Viewing (Pty) Ltd. t/a SV Consulting is a specialist service provider operating within the mining and exploration industries. Our core business activities focus on the following areas:

Geological Services:

We offer a comprehensive range of geological support, including mineral exploration, geological mapping and resource evaluation. Our experienced geologist assists mining companies in understanding and optimizing their mineral assets.

Project Support Services:

We provide essential support for mining and exploration projects, including project management, field operations logistics, site supervision, data management, and technical reporting. We ensure projects are completed on schedule, within budget, and to the highest professional standards.

Drone Operations and Aerial Surveys:

We are licensed drone operators specialising in the mining sector. Our services include aerial photography, blast monitoring, infrastructure inspections, and environmental monitoring. Our drone technology delivers high-resolution, real-time data, enabling clients to make informed operational decisions.


All our operations are carried out in strict compliance with industry regulations and best practices, ensuring safety, efficiency, and environmental responsibility.


We remain committed to providing innovative, cost-effective solutions that add value to our clients' operations.
